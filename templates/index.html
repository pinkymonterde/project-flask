Our vision is to create a literate and empowered nation

where each individual, regardless of age or environment, has the opportunity
to acquire and utilize literacy skills to improve their quality of life and contribute
to the socio-economic development of the country

We aim to bridge the gap between the literacy rate of the regions in the country

and identify key factors influencing literacy levels to implement evidence-based strategies that address literacy gaps.
